Frequently asked questions

Registered patrons can:
  • Login to Wi-Fi
  • Download eBooks or eResources (from anywhere, not just in the library)
  • Borrow print books and journals
  • Use public computers (terminals) in the building
  • Use self-service print/copy/scan machines

Anyone older than 15 years of age with a valid passport/ID.

Your username and password are required to:
  • Login to Wi-Fi
  • Access your user account.

The opening hours are from 9am till 8pm everyday.

Your financial account can be charged from home (via online payment or bank transfer) or at the Cash Desk on the ground floor (open only during Patron Services hours).

Login to your user account in the top right-hand corner of the website. Insert the username and password you selected when you registered.

Search using the catalog or ask our information professionals during Patron Services hours.

You can use the self-check machines or a counter with a Patron.

There may be several reasons. Ask our library staff for assistance:
  • The item is available only for study in the library. Usually these are popular items used by many patrons.
  • You have an outstanding fee.
  • Your registration is going to expire soon.
